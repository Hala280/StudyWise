✦ Who We Are

Revolutionizing Student Productivity

StudyWise isn't just another to-do list. We bridge the gap between heavy academic workloads and the limited hours in a student's day using state-of-the-art AI algorithms.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize access to smart educational tools. We want to eliminate study burnout by providing students with an intelligent engine that organizes their academic life automatically, letting them focus solely on learning.

👁️

Our Vision

To become the global standard for academic time management. We envision a future where no student fails a class or misses a deadline simply because of poor planning or overwhelming schedules.

✦ Core Engine

The Anatomy of StudyWise

Discover exactly how every feature is engineered to guarantee your academic success.

Step 1: Data Extraction

AI Syllabus Parser

Forget manual data entry. Upload your PDF syllabus, and our Azure-backed AI instantly extracts critical dates, assignment weights, and lecture topics.

  • Extracts reading materials and deadlines
  • Identifies exam dates automatically
  • Creates structured DB records in seconds
Step 2: Processing

Constraint-Based Scheduler

Our C# Interval Scheduling Algorithm acts as your personal manager. You input when you are busy (classes, work), and the engine finds the optimal gaps for studying.

  • Prevents scheduling conflicts automatically
  • Balances heavy and light subjects
  • Respects your personal "off" hours
Step 3: Real-time Adaptation

Adaptive "Life Happens" Logic

Missed a day? Got sick? No problem. The system dynamically shifts your remaining workload across future available slots without overlapping existing tasks.

  • Real-time background recalculation
  • No guilt-tripping for missed tasks
  • Maintains long-term exam readiness
Step 4: Insights

Progress Analytics

Visualize your academic health. Our complex LINQ queries feed beautiful dashboards showing you exactly where you stand, preventing burnout through gamification.

  • Study velocity tracking
  • Subject heatmaps to spot weaknesses
  • Streak counters to build consistency
